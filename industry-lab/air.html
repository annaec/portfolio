ABOUT

Industry Lab is a community-driven coworking space where artists, entreprenuers, and inventors gather together to work, play, and learn. Our seasonal Artist in Residence Program invites makers of all stripes to work in a generous portion of dedicated space, interact with a dynamic community, and curate an exhibit in our gallery.
